Welcome to UNC ASDA

Empowering future dental professionals through leadership, advocacy, and service

We are the UNC Chapter of the American Student Dental Association at the Adams School of Dentistry.

The American Student Dental Association (ASDA) is a national, student-led organization that protects and advances the rights, interests, and well-being of dental students. It connects students with organized dentistry and provides education, resources, representation, and advocacy.

For more than 75 years, the UNC Adams School of Dentistry has prepared outstanding oral health professionals for North Carolina and beyond.

At UNC ASDA, we bring that mission to life by fostering leadership, service, community, and professional development to create the best possible dental school experience for our members.
